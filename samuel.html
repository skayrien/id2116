I am Samuel Koh, and I am a year 2 Industrial Designer in NUS. I am inclined to service design and wish to use design to make a change. I wish to ultimately create a studio which aims to help to improve the lives of people tangibly via groundbreaking yet simple ideas.

My strengthes as a designer are...

  • an ability to understand people
  • service to people
  • willingness to learn
  • drive to succeed
